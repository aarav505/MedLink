How MedLink Works

Making medicine donation simple, safe, and accessible for everyone

User Registration & Verification

Users register with email and phone number, receiving an OTP via SMS for two-factor authentication, ensuring safe transactions between users.

Security First:

Our two-factor authentication ensures that all user accounts are secure and verified, preventing fraud and establishing trust between donors and recipients.

Medicine Listing & Verification

Donors list medicines with images and expiry dates. A qualified pharmacist reviews each listing to verify safety before public display.

Quality Assurance:

Every medicine undergoes thorough verification by licensed pharmacists who check expiration dates, packaging integrity, and compliance with health regulations.

Medicine Ordering System

Users search for needed medicines, view local availability, and place orders directly through our secure platform.

Simplified Access:

Our search engine helps users quickly find needed medicines in their locality, connecting donors with recipients efficiently through our secure platform.

Community Engagement & Education

Weekly newsletters provide awareness about new diseases and prevention methods, building a supportive community committed to public health.

Health Education:

Beyond medicine redistribution, we provide valuable health information through newsletters, building a community focused on improved health outcomes.

Join MedLink Today

Be part of our mission to reduce medicine waste and increase accessibility to those in need. Together we can make healthcare more accessible and equitable for everyone.
